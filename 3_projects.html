MMI-401: Audio Electronics Final Project



This is my culminating project for the Audio Electronics course at UM. Essentially, I modeled the "VOCO-LOCO" Mic Preamp and Effects Loop pedal by creating my own digital circuitry schematics, tested them with a simulation based circuitry diagnostic & testing software, and then proved the theoretical mathematics behind each components values and their roles within the schematic. The in-depth research can be viewed in document above. You can click here to see the PDF if it does not automatically load to the page.
